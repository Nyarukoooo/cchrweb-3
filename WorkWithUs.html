Work With Us

Careers

Job

Work for CCHR as a staff member in one of our paid positions.

National Internships

Join CCHR through our strong Cambodian Internship Program aimed at providing Cambodian university students embarking into the workplace for the first time with an opportunity to gain valuable work experience in an NGO.

International Internships

Join CCHR’s international internship program if you are a graduate with knowledge of international human rights law and mechanisms, with a strong interest in Cambodia.

Professional Volunteers

Volunteer with CCHR if you are a professional in the field of law or human rights who can assist CCHR through the application of your professional experience and skills.
