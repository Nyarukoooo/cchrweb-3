Donate Now
Your support can help us conduct crucial research, advocacy and campaigning activities aimed at affecting concrete human rights change in Cambodia. Your help can also support us in empowering local communities and activists throughout Cambodia who are fighting for their rights. Your contribution will allow us to plan our projects and our work more efficiently, and will contribute to improving the human rights situation in Cambodia.

Your help is crucial to support ...

Every day courageous human rights defenders take huge risks in their work to promote and protect human rights in Cambodia as they fight for better respect of their and their communities’ rights. By donating to the Cambodian Center for Human Rights you will empower those whose rights are violated and support the ordinary people, grassroots activists and human rights defenders who fight for the free and peaceful exercise of fundamental freedoms, legislative reform, equality before the law, respect for human rights by businesses, and equality for women, LGBTIQ individuals, and other marginalized groups. At CCHR we advocate for the progressive realization of human rights by promoting and protecting the civil and political rights of inspiring activists and civil society. Your contribution will help us to work towards rule of law and democracy in Cambodia, for the benefit of all Cambodians.
